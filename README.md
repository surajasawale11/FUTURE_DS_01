# Business Sales Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Business Sales Performance Dashboard** developed using **Power BI**. The dashboard analyzes sales data to identify revenue trends, product performance, regional profitability, and customer segment contributions. It enables businesses to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) and make informed, data-driven decisions.

---

## Project Objectives

* Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
* Identify top-selling products and categories.
* Evaluate regional sales and profitability.
* Monitor monthly sales trends.
* Analyze customer segment performance.
* Provide actionable business recommendations.

---

## Dataset

* **Dataset:** Superstore Sales Dataset
* **Source:** Kaggle
* **Format:** CSV

---

## Tools & Technologies

* Power BI Desktop
* Power Query
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Microsoft Excel / CSV

---

## Data Preparation

The dataset was cleaned and transformed using Power Query by:

* Removing duplicate records
* Handling missing values
* Correcting data types
* Creating Year, Month, Quarter, and Month Name columns
* Standardizing text values
* Removing unnecessary columns

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Profit Margin (%)

---

## Dashboard Features

* KPI Cards
* Monthly Sales Trend (Line Chart)
* Sales by Category (Column Chart)
* Profit by Region (Bar Chart)
* Sales by Segment (Donut Chart)
* Top 10 Products by Sales
* Sales vs. Profit (Scatter Chart)
* Interactive Slicers (Year, Region, Category, Segment)

---

## Key Insights

* Sales performance can be tracked over time to identify business trends.
* Product analysis highlights the highest revenue-generating products.
* Regional analysis identifies the most profitable locations.
* Customer segment analysis helps understand revenue contribution.
* Profitability analysis supports better business decision-making.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Increase focus on high-performing products and categories.
* Improve sales strategies in lower-performing regions.
* Monitor products with high sales but low profit margins.
* Strengthen engagement with profitable customer segments.
* Use sales trends to improve inventory and business planning.

---

## Conclusion

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business sales performance by combining key metrics, trend analysis, and interactive visualizations. It enables stakeholders to evaluate sales, profit, product performance, regional results, and customer segments in one place. The insights generated from this analysis can support strategic decision-making, improve operational efficiency, and help identify opportunities for sustainable business growth.
